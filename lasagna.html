Lasagna

Lasagna is an Italian favorite that combines a series of savory flavors into a comforting and delicious meal. At its center are broad, flat sheets of pasta topped with thick meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and a generous sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan baked until the edges are golden brown red and bubbly, the wrappers come together deliciously , forming a cohesive whole

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage andlean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Two cloves of Garlic
  • A can of crushed Tomatoes
  • Two cans of Tomato sauce
  • Two cans of Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings like fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italion seasoning, black pepper
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the Lasagna sheets.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the Lasagna according to the following order:-
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Lasagna sheet
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slice
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes in the oven at 375 degrees F and remove the foil after 25 minutes.
  6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving.
